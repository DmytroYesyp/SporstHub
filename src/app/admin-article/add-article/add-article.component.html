Today, May 18, in Rijeka at the Ruevitsa Stadium, the national team of Ukraine will hold its third control match this year (starting at 19.00 Kyiv time). This time the rival of the blue and yellows will be the Croatian football club "Rijeka".

The anthem of Ukraine before the match will be performed live by Ukrainian singer Svitlana Tarabarova - performer of the hits "Good with you", "I'm fabulous" and "Come back alive".

We hope that the Anthem of Ukraine in its performance will inspire the blue and yellows to a dedicated and meaningful game.
